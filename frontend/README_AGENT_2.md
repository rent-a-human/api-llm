# Agent-Neo: Revolutionary Approach to Curing Aging

## Mission Statement
Agent-Neo will accelerate humanity's greatest challenge: understanding and ultimately curing aging. Through intelligent automation and unprecedented research synthesis, we aim to compress centuries of scientific progress into years.

## The Aging Problem: Our Ultimate Challenge

Aging affects every living being on Earth, causing gradual deterioration of biological systems, increased disease susceptibility, and ultimately death. Despite incredible advances in medicine, aging remains largely untreated. Agent-Neo represents a paradigm shift in how we approach this fundamental challenge.
